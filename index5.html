The Club

C.F. Real Madrid

Real Madrid is a professional Spanish football team founded in 1902,headquartered in the Spanish capital Madrid.
The team plays in the Spanish league and was named the best football team of the 20th century, winning the league 33 times,
19 times the King's Cup and scoring a record 13 Champions League titles.
Coach: Santiago Solari .
Nicknames: Los Blancos (The Whites); Los Merengues (The Meringues); Los Vikingos (The Vikings) .
Venue / Playground: Santiago Bernabeu Stadium .
Chief Executive Officer: José Angel Sanchez (May 25, 2011) .
Periodicals: La Liga, UEFA Champions League, European Super Cup, Club World Cup, King's Cup, Spanish Super .
