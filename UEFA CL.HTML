UEFA Champions League

Real Madrid is the most successful club in Champions League history having with 13 championships including all five in its first five seasons.
The Champions League replaced the European Cup in 1992, which had been run since 1955. The club has won seven titles since the Champions League format was introduced.
The team has won four of the last five titles — in 2018, 2017, 2016 and 2014. Real Madrid star Cristiano Ronaldo has won five Champions League titles — four with Real Madrid and one with Manchester United.
